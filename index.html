UPVC Spray Painting Services

We offer the highest quality UPVC spray painting services for your home.

Paint my Gaff are your local, trusted UPVC painters. We’re experts in all aspects of UPVC spray painting. We’re well-established and widely recommended by our customers, and we stamp our work with a 10-year guarantee.

We can spray paint all internal and external UPVC on your home.

We paint windows, doors, door frames, fascia, garage doors, conservatories and everything in-between. If you are thinking about painting UPVC windows to save money, it works out at around 75% cheaper than replacement without the inconvenience of building work.
